about us

what makes our coffee special?

Taste and See Coffee Shop and Gallery offers a unique coffee house environment unlike any other in Macon. We are not only a place to drop in and get your morning cup of coffee are a place where you can sit down and enjoy that tailor-made cup of coffee. We offer a delicious variety of coffee from Safehouse Coffee made by our professionally trained baristas. We have everything from classic coffee to our house made specialty beverages. All of our sauces & syrups are made in-house with all natural ingredients (no chemicals or preservatives) ensuring you the highest quality in taste & health. You can complete your coffee with one of our delicious sweet treats made by our very own baker.
We look forward to serving you at Taste & See Coffee Shop and Gallery!
